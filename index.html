Why we exist

Only 1% of young refugees access higher education.

If their lives hadn’t been disrupted, over 30% of them would have.

They aren’t able to contribute towards and integrate as well with their host country, economically or socially and aren’t as qualified as they could be to help reconstruct their country and make it as safe and stable a place.

Losing your home shouldn’t mean losing your ability to learn and make the world a better place.

We use technology and academic partnerships to empower local community organisations in assembling online learning.
