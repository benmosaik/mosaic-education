About us

We believe that refugees, asylum seekers, displaced people and conflict-affected communities should be able to access, shape and lead the education they require for rebuilding their societies or for integration and success in new ones.

Our values

Community lead

Empowering communities affected by conflict and displacement to participate in the design and delivery of our programmes, acknowledging that resilience is best achieved by building community capacity.

Human Centred

Striving to create opportunities for those who are most disadvantaged and marginalised.

Collaborative

Incorporating others' ideas and sharing our own, acknowledging that they are improved through modification and reinvention by others.

Always learning

Always looking to reflect, seek feedback and improve what we do and how we work alongside the beneficiaries of our programmes.
