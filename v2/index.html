Agora Education

Supporting refugees to reassemble their educational lives.

We use technology and academic partnerships to empower local community organisations in assembling online learning.

Why we exist

Only 1% of young refugees access higher education. If their lives hadn’t been disrupted, over 30% of them would have.

They aren’t able to contribute towards and integrate as well with their host country, economically or socially and aren’t as qualified as they could be to help reconstruct their country and make it as safe and stable a place.

Losing your home shouldn’t mean losing your ability to learn and make the world a better place.

How we work

We design and develop programmes alongside refugee communities, and build partnerships with local civil society organisations (CSOs).

To reach marginalised refugee students we use a combination of digital content, connected learning systems and social media to deliver programmes.

We partner with education institutions to leverage high quality content and programmes to support refugees accessing university.
